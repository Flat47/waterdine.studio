Evil Shogun™
- Revenge of the Samurai -

"Bring me the sword of Koyama."

Evil Shogun™ is our first iOS mobile game. The fictitious story is set during the Japanese Shogunate period, when the Shogun had strong power over the Emperor. Along with the far east oriental story, we created several types of puzzles for the players to solve.
